Ophira, a manifestation of living in an entropic world. An elk, infected by a fungal parasite. Her hulking body, tattered & decayed, provides a twisted playground of bone & flame. Pressing through her flesh reveals a macabre grotto, filled with light & a nightmarish soundscape to transfix your senses

- Description -

A decaying elk slowly roams.

A large geometric head encases her visible skull. Her exposed ribcage and (intentionally) tattered skins draw participants towards her. Her legs are tired but her antlers are bright. From the exterior one can see a pulsating veining, a hint of what her interior will expose. A fistula on the side of her body both beckons and deters. For those willing to enter it, an interior light and soundscape are found within. Thick and spidering veining has taken over inside this body cavity. Custom built, upholstered ‘caves’ and ‘benches’ can be explored, but be mindful, not all is as it seems. This slightest move within her can cause havoc or serenity.

Become entirely immersed in her, and by her.

This is Ophira.

- Ophira Experiential Arts -

OEA is a Toronto based artist collective with members from various disciplines, backgrounds & experiences.

Composed of fire & scenic artists, fabricators, programmers, games designers, film makers, metalsmith's, and professional upholsterers.
